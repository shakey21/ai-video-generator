# Content Brief: Urban Dance Sequence

## Concept
Modern urban dance routine with smooth transitions and high-energy moves.

## Target Duration
30 seconds

## Movement Style
- Modern/contemporary fusion
- High energy, athletic
- Smooth transitions
- Strong finish pose

## Choreography Notes
1. Opening: Attention-grabbing isolation (3s)
2. Build-up: Flowing arm movements transitioning to footwork (12s)
3. Peak: Fast-paced combination, jumps/spins (10s)
4. Finale: Strong pose with attitude (5s)

## Energy Level
High (8/10) - Maintain momentum throughout

## Scene Preference
Urban studio with dramatic lighting, minimal background

## Music Sync
Align peak moves with beat drops (if background music used)

- Camera: Static medium shot
- Lighting: High contrast, dramatic shadows
